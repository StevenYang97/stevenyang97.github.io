Design Philosophy

My personal design philosophy is to promote cultural diversity in the designing field and to make the users of different cultures or sub-culture able to not only like the product because it is easy to use and efficient, but also love them because they can resonate with the product. The user should feel happy not only to use the product but also to live with it. Instead of just focusing on being an “efficient” product, the designing outcomes should focus on fitting harmoniously within the target users’ personal lives taking into consideration that there are many kinds of living styles created by many cultures and sub-cultures.
